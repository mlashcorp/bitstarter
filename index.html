The smart surveillance system

Skaut is an inexpensive cloud-enabled surveillance system, that uses state-of-the-art machine learning technology to be the best watchdog you ever had.

Keep me in the loop

Powered by raspberry pi

A raspberry pi is at the heart of the skaut. This inexpensive ARM platform provides the necessary horsepower for all the neat tricks we do, such as facial recognition, on-the-fly video transcoding, image enhancement, person or pet detector, and many more to come

Smart

Most home surveillance cameras are simply triggered by motion. With skaut, we want to do better, and only warn you when something meaningful happens.

Affordable

Off-the-shelf components help keep the price down

Stylish

A surveillance camera you can keep on your coffe table.

Always connected

Power and wifi are the two things the skaut needs to thrive.

All seeing

Near 360º viewing angles allow you to place the skaut nearly anywhere in your home.

1 step installation

Just launch the app in your smartphone, and show the registration QR code to the skaut. Voila.
